Cee PRIVACY POLICY

At Orbital we care about your privacy. The purpose of this privacy policy is to be transparent and help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it and how we manage your information.

Why do we process your personal data?

At Orbital we process the information you provide us in order to offer you the Cee services described in the Terms And Condition Of Use Of Cee Through our advertising partners, we can also offer you advertising according to the information that you provide or we collect through the use of Cee.

How do we collect your data?

You provide the data when you register or use Cee. When you use any of the services offered by Cee, information is collected regarding the use thereof and the device on which it is installed. Another data controller provides us your information so you can use our services. All the collected information is used to maintain and improve the quality of the provided service. Thanks to this information, Cee can offer customized services, improve the app and solve operating errors. We also use your information to communicate with you through messages containing information about updates, promotions or news pertaining to the app. In the event you do not approve our requests to use some or all of your personal data, it is possible that some of the functions or services of Cee may not be available.

How long do we store your data?

The personal data provided shall be stored according to the following principles:
  • The corresponding required legal period.
  • The data subject gives their consent.
  • For filing claims and lawsuits.
  • To protect your rights.
  • Because we have a contract in force with you.

What legitimizes the processing of your data?

  • The data subject’s consent: you have consented to one or more specific processes. Before starting this processing we will have requested your consent.
  • Legitimate interest: when the processing is done within the framework of the relationship with you so we can offer you customized services.
  • Execution of a contract: when we have a contract with you and need data to establish that relationship.
  • Legal compliance: when processing data is necessary in order to comply with the laws and regulations in force.

Who will we send your data to?

  • Identifying data:Common personal data may include personal identification data, such as your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, photograph, date of birth, IP address and other similar information classified as basic.
  • Browsing data:These data are sent anonymously so they cannot be traced back to the identifying data. The data we collect include: latitude, longitude, time, GPS accuracy, speed and direction of movement.
  • Usage data:These data are sent anonymously to improve the services we provide. We collect the following data: the location where the app is downloaded, frequency of app use, time of use, device type and model, unique identifiers, language, operating system version and battery use. We also collect information on malfunction and performance reports.

Are your data processed outside the EU?

If Orbital processors, technological partners or advertising partners process information outside the EU, such data would fall under the “Privacy Shield”, binding corporate regulations and contractual clauses. In the case of data controllers, you must consult their privacy policies to know exactly what they do with the data you provide them. You can do so by clicking on the privacy links we have provided above.

What are your rights when you provide us data?

Everyone has the right to receive confirmation as to whether Orbital is processing their personal data.

  • Data subjects have the right to access their personal data, as well as request the rectification of any inaccurate data or, where appropriate, request same be omitted when, among other reasons, the data are no longer necessary for the purposes for which they were collected.
  • Under certain circumstances, data subjects may request the processing of their data be limited, in which case we will only store same for filing or defending against claims.
  • Under certain circumstances and for reasons pertaining to their specific situation, data subjects may oppose the processing of their data. Orbital will cease to process the data, except where there are compelling legitimate reasons and for filing or defending against potential claims.

You have the following rights:

  • Right to request access to the personal data pertaining to the data subject
  • Right to request the rectification or omission thereof
  • Right to request the processing be limited
  • Right to oppose the processing
  • Right to data portability.

Your exercise of these rights is subject to certain exceptions for public interest reasons (for example, the prevention and detection of crimes) and for our own interests (for example, maintaining legal advice confidential). If you were to exercise any of these rights and we confirm that you are legitimately empowered to do so, we will respond within one month or the maximum period envisaged in Iraqi regulations, whichever is shorter.

If you are not satisfied with how we use your information or with our response to your exercising any of these rights, you can file a claim before the Iraqi Data Protection Agency
